# Research — April 2026

## 1. [Sony AI's table-tennis agent reaches elite human level — cover of Nature](https://ai.sony/news/sony-ai-announces-breakthrough-research-in-real-world-artificial-intelligence-and-robotics)
**Source:** Sony AI · **Date:** 2026-04-23
