# Industry — April 2026

## 1. [Google plans to invest up to $40 billion in Anthropic at a $350B valuation](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-24/google-plans-to-invest-up-to-40-billion-in-anthropic)
**Source:** Bloomberg · **Date:** 2026-04-24

Why this matters: $10B upfront, $30B more tied to performance milestones, plus 5 gigawatts of Google Cloud compute over five years — at a $350B valuation. The deal cements two things: (1) Anthropic is now structurally a Google-and-Amazon-funded competitor to OpenAI, and (2) compute commitments, not cash, are the real currency of frontier AI deals. Google's anxiety about losing the AI-coding market to Claude Code drove the timing.

## 2. [Google Cloud launches the Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform](https://cloud.google.com/blog/products/ai-machine-learning/introducing-gemini-enterprise-agent-platform)
**Source:** Google Cloud Blog · **Date:** 2026-04-22

Why this matters: Vertex AI is being subsumed into a unified agent platform covering build, governance, orchestration, and security. Google is staking out the position that the enterprise battle isn't model-vs-model but platform-vs-platform — and trying to box in Microsoft Foundry and AWS Bedrock before they consolidate.

## 3. [EY rolls out enterprise-scale agentic AI inside its audit practice globally](https://www.ey.com/en_gl/newsroom/2026/04/ey-launches-enterprise-scale-agentic-ai-to-redefine-the-audit-experience-for-the-ai-era)
**Source:** EY · **Date:** 2026-04-07

Why this matters: A Big Four firm embedding a multi-agent framework directly into its core audit workflow (EY Canvas, on Microsoft Foundry/Fabric) is one of the most consequential real-world deployments yet. Audit is regulated, high-liability work — if it sticks here, the case for agentic AI in professional services is essentially settled.

## 4. [ServiceNow and Accenture launch a Forward Deployed Engineering program for agentic AI](https://newsroom.accenture.com/news/2026/servicenow-and-accenture-launch-forward-deployed-engineering-program-to-scale-agentic-ai-across-the-enterprise)
**Source:** Accenture · **Date:** 2026-04

Why this matters: The "Forward Deployed Engineer" model — pioneered by Palantir and adopted by OpenAI — is now standard practice at the systems-integrator scale. ServiceNow + Accenture means a global rollout pipeline aimed at Fortune 500 ops, replacing the "buy a SaaS seat" model with embedded human-AI delivery teams.

## 5. [Writer launches autonomous AI agents that act without prompts](https://venturebeat.com/technology/writer-launches-ai-agents-that-can-act-without-prompts-taking-on-amazon-microsoft-and-salesforce)
**Source:** VentureBeat · **Date:** 2026-04

Why this matters: Writer's agents take initiative on their own based on triggers and goals, not user prompts. It's a credibility test for the "agents that watch your inbox / your data / your queue and act" thesis — and a direct challenge to Salesforce Agentforce, Microsoft Copilot, and Amazon Q at the application layer.
