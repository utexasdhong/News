## 4. [Trump fires all 22 members of the National Science Board](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_in_climate_change)
**Source:** Wikipedia (2026 in climate change) · **Date:** 2026-04

Why this matters: The NSB, established in 1950, guides the NSF and advises the president and Congress on science policy. Wholesale removal of all 22 members is unprecedented and signals continued institutional restructuring of federal science governance — with downstream effects on climate funding, AI research grants, and university overhead policy.
