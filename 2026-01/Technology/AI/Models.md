# Models — January 2026

## 1. [Gemini 3 becomes the default model for Google AI Overviews](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/products/google-ai-updates-january-2026/)
**Source:** Google Blog · **Date:** 2026-01

Why this matters: Gemini 3 shipped "Agentic Vision" — the model actively explores images rather than processing them once — and became the engine behind AI Overviews for every Google search globally. This pushes multimodal reasoning from research demo to default consumer experience at Google's scale.

## 2. [Google launches Gemini 3.1 Ultra with native multimodal training and 2M-token context](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/products/google-ai-updates-january-2026/)
**Source:** Google Blog · **Date:** 2026-01

Why this matters: Unlike prior versions that bolted modalities on, 3.1 Ultra was trained from scratch to reason across text, image, audio, and video simultaneously, with a built-in sandboxed code-execution tool. It signals the start of "natively multimodal" as the new baseline for frontier models.

## 3. [NVIDIA releases Cosmos and GR00T open models for robot reasoning at CES](https://investor.nvidia.com/news/press-release-details/2026/NVIDIA-Releases-New-Physical-AI-Models-as-Global-Partners-Unveil-Next-Generation-Robots/default.aspx)
**Source:** NVIDIA Investor Relations · **Date:** 2026-01-05

Why this matters: NVIDIA pushed "physical AI" — open foundation models specifically for robotics learning — into the public release pipeline alongside Isaac Lab-Arena and the OSMO compute framework. It's the clearest signal yet that the next big AI deployment surface is hardware in the physical world, not just chatbots.

## 4. [Anthropic ships Claude Opus 4.6](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-6)
**Source:** Anthropic · **Date:** 2026-01

Why this matters: Anthropic kept its rapid release cadence into 2026 with Opus 4.6, focused on agentic coding and long-horizon task reliability. The two-week release rhythm itself is a story: frontier labs have moved from "annual big drops" to continuous iteration.
