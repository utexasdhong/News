# Crypto & Fintech — January 2026

## 1. [Bitcoin pushes above $90,000 to open the year, briefly touches $97K](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/01/02/bitcoin-pushes-above-usd90-000-as-traders-eye-change-in-pattern)

Why this matters: BTC opened 2026 strong on new-year allocations and a haven bid from geopolitical tensions (Venezuela, Iran). Mid-month it touched $97-98K with MicroStrategy and ETF flows providing alternating support — but ended the month down 4.8%.

## 2. [Bitcoin spot ETFs see $1.6B in January net outflows](https://www.talos.com/insights/state-of-the-network-348)
**Source:** Talos (State of the Network) · **Date:** 2026-01

Why this matters: First major monthly outflow from spot BTC ETFs in a while — Ethereum ETFs lost $343M too. Solana ETFs took in $104M and XRP ETFs $15M, signaling rotation within crypto rather than wholesale flight. ETF flow data is now the cleanest read on institutional crypto sentiment.

## 3. [Wintermute declares "the four-year crypto cycle is dead"](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/01/19/here-are-the-3-big-things-that-will-move-bitcoin-and-crypto-prices-in-2026)
**Source:** CoinDesk · **Date:** 2026-01

Why this matters: One of the largest crypto market makers publicly arguing the halving-driven cycle no longer dominates price action. If correct, it reshapes how funds, builders, and retail size positions — the "wait for the next cycle" thesis stops being a reliable strategy.

## 4. [Senate Agriculture Committee advances crypto market structure bill](https://crypto.com/us/research/market-update-jan-2026)
**Source:** Crypto.com Research · **Date:** 2026-01

Why this matters: The bill cleared committee on a party-line vote — moving the US closer to its first comprehensive federal crypto market structure framework. Once law, this resolves the years-long fight over which agency regulates which token, and reshapes US crypto markets in a single stroke.

## 5. [Total digital asset market cap touches $3.4T to open 2026](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/01/06/here-s-why-bitcoin-and-major-tokens-are-seeing-a-strong-start-to-2026)
