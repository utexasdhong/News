## 2. [Nvidia's Groq 3 LPU enters production as part of the Vera Rubin family](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-vera-rubin-platform)
**Source:** NVIDIA Newsroom · **Date:** 2026-05

Why this matters: Following the GTC 2026 unveiling, the Vera Rubin platform — seven chips including the new Groq 3 LPU integrated alongside Rubin GPUs, NVLink 6 switches, BlueField-4 DPUs, and ConnectX-9 SuperNICs — moved into full production rollout through May. The Groq 3 LPX rack paired with Vera Rubin NVL72 is being marketed as delivering 35x higher throughput per megawatt, a critical metric as data-center power becomes the binding constraint.
