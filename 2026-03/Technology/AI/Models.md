# Models — March 2026

## 1. [OpenAI releases GPT-5.4 with record computer-use benchmarks](https://renovateqr.com/blog/ai-model-releases-2026)
**Source:** Renovate QR (industry roundup) · **Date:** 2026-03-05

Why this matters: GPT-5.4 posted record scores on OSWorld-Verified and WebArena Verified — the two benchmarks that actually measure whether a model can drive a browser and OS like a human — and hit 83% on OpenAI's own GDPval test for knowledge work. Computer-use is now the frontier where the labs are competing, not chat quality.

## 2. [NVIDIA unveils Vera Rubin platform and updated open models at GTC 2026](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/gtc-2026-news/)
**Source:** NVIDIA Blog · **Date:** 2026-03-17

Why this matters: Vera Rubin — 1.3 million components, 10x perf/watt over Grace Blackwell — sets the substrate for the next generation of frontier models, while NVIDIA's open NeMo and physical-AI model updates expand the open-weights ecosystem that smaller labs build on. The "platform owns the models" strategy continued.

## 3. [Anthropic's Claude 4.6 rollout completes with multi-agent Claude Code](https://aizolo.com/blog/top-ai-models-march-2026/)
**Source:** AIzolo (industry roundup) · **Date:** 2026-03

Why this matters: With Opus 4.6 (Feb 5) and Sonnet 4.6 (Feb 17) now fully diffused into developer tools through March, Anthropic introduced multi-agent parallelism in Claude Code — multiple Claude instances coordinating on different parts of the same codebase. Reports put Anthropic at over half of the enterprise coding market on the back of this release.

## 4. [Google's Gemini 3.1 Pro consolidates lead on general-purpose benchmarks](https://renovateqr.com/blog/ai-model-releases-2026)
**Source:** Renovate QR · **Date:** 2026-03

Why this matters: A month after launch, Gemini 3.1 Pro dominated 13 of 16 major benchmarks and became the default reference point for "best general-purpose model." With GPT-5.4 strong on agentic tasks and Claude on coding, the trifecta of frontier leaders is now visibly stratified by use case rather than raw capability.

## 5. [Sakana AI's "AI Scientist v2" published in Nature](https://theconversation.com/the-ai-scientist-now-academic-papers-can-be-fully-automated-what-does-this-mean-for-the-future-of-research-282161)
**Source:** The Conversation · **Date:** 2026-03

Why this matters: The first peer-reviewed account of a system that autonomously generates hypotheses, runs experiments, analyzes data, and writes complete papers — one of which was already accepted at ICLR 2025. Frontier model capability is now a research-production model, not just a chat product.
