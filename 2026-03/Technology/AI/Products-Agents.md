# Products & Agents — March 2026

## 1. [NVIDIA launches Agent Toolkit and OpenShell runtime at GTC 2026](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/ai-agents)
**Source:** NVIDIA Newsroom · **Date:** 2026-03-17

Why this matters: NVIDIA's Agent Toolkit packages open-source models, policy-based security guardrails, and a sandbox runtime for autonomous enterprise agents. OpenShell — an open runtime enforcing network and privacy policy on tool calls — is the missing piece between "agents are cool" and "agents are safe to deploy in regulated companies."

## 2. [Writer rolls out Skills and proactive agents that act without prompts](https://venturebeat.com/technology/writer-launches-ai-agents-that-can-act-without-prompts-taking-on-amazon-microsoft-and-salesforce)
**Source:** VentureBeat · **Date:** 2026-03

Why this matters: Writer's "Skills" let teams encode methodologies and decision frameworks into reusable agent blocks, plus the agents can now trigger themselves on events. Direct shot at Microsoft Copilot Studio, Salesforce Agentforce, and Amazon's enterprise stack — and a useful indicator that the agent-platform war is now about workflow ergonomics, not raw model quality.

## 3. [Siemens debuts Fuse EDA AI Agent at GTC 2026](https://news.siemens.com/en-us/siemens-fuse-eda-ai-agent/)
**Source:** Siemens Newsroom · **Date:** 2026-03-17

Why this matters: Fuse EDA orchestrates the full chip-design workflow with autonomous agents — the first major industrial-grade application of agentic AI inside electronic design automation. If it works, it shortens chip-design cycles measurably and proves agents can run a regulated, mission-critical engineering process.

## 4. [IBM watsonx Orchestrate gets ElevenLabs voice integration](https://newsroom.ibm.com/2026-03-25-enterprise-ai-finds-its-voice-elevenlabs-and-ibm-bring-premium-voice-capabilities-to-agentic-ai)
**Source:** IBM Newsroom · **Date:** 2026-03-25

Why this matters: Voice agents move from consumer toy (ChatGPT Advanced Voice) to enterprise orchestration layer. IBM brings the compliance and integration story; ElevenLabs brings the voice quality — a credible enterprise voice-agent stack is now shippable.

## 5. [Microsoft + NVIDIA fold Foundry into joint Grace Blackwell cloud](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/gtc-2026-news/)
**Source:** NVIDIA Blog · **Date:** 2026-03-17

Why this matters: Microsoft Foundry — Azure's AI/agent layer — gets first-class access to NVIDIA open models running on hundreds of thousands of liquid-cooled Grace Blackwell GPUs across Microsoft's data centers. The packaging matters: enterprise customers can now deploy NVIDIA's agent stack through their existing Azure procurement.
