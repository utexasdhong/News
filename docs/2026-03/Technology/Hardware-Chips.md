# Hardware & Chips — March 2026

## 1. [NVIDIA unveils Vera Rubin platform at GTC 2026 keynote](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/16/nvidia-gtc-2026-ceo-jensen-huang-keynote-blackwell-vera-rubin.html)
**Source:** CNBC · **Date:** 2026-03-16

Why this matters: Vera Rubin — 1.3 million components, 10x performance-per-watt over Grace Blackwell — ships later in 2026 and anchors NVIDIA's $1 trillion roadmap of Blackwell + Vera Rubin orders through 2027. Jensen Huang's stated order book sets the capacity ceiling that every other AI infrastructure decision (data centers, energy, custom silicon) is now planning against.

## 2. [Intel introduces Clearwater Forest, its first 18A server CPU](https://www.tomshardware.com/tech-industry/semiconductors/intel-chip-roadmap-2026-2028)
**Source:** Tom's Hardware · **Date:** 2026-03-03

Why this matters: Clearwater Forest debuted at MWC with up to 288 Darkmont E-cores on 18A — Intel's first server chip on its leading-edge node, and the proof point its foundry strategy needs before customers commit to 14A. After years of node-leadership loss to TSMC, this is Intel's biggest credibility test of the decade.

## 3. [NVIDIA's agentic-AI pivot drives chip demand narrative at GTC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/nvidia-gtc-2026-agentic-ai-chips-tech-download.html)
**Source:** CNBC · **Date:** 2026-03-20

Why this matters: Huang explicitly recast the chip-demand story around agentic AI — agents spawn other agents, so inference volume grows non-linearly with deployment. This reframes the entire investment thesis for AI infrastructure away from "training scale" toward "inference at agent-multiplier scale."

## 4. [Microsoft deploys hundreds of thousands of Grace Blackwell GPUs globally](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/gtc-2026-news/)
**Source:** NVIDIA Blog · **Date:** 2026-03-17

Why this matters: Microsoft committed to liquid-cooled Grace Blackwell deployments across its global data centers and tied Microsoft Foundry to NVIDIA's open-model stack. The "Microsoft owns the customer, NVIDIA owns the silicon" partnership solidified — bad news for AMD's Instinct line and for hyperscaler custom-silicon ambitions in the near term.

## 5. [Uber + NVIDIA announce 28-city Drive AV deployment by 2028](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/16/nvidia-gtc-2026-ceo-jensen-huang-keynote-blackwell-vera-rubin.html)
**Source:** CNBC · **Date:** 2026-03-16

Why this matters: Uber commits to a Drive AV-powered fleet across 28 cities on four continents starting LA and San Francisco; Nissan, BYD, Geely, Isuzu, and Hyundai signed on for Level-4 vehicles using NVIDIA's Drive Hyperion. Robotaxi has been "next year" for a decade — March is when the rollout actually got dated.
