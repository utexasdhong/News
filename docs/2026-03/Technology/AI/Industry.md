# Industry — March 2026

## 1. [OpenAI announces $122B funding round at $852B valuation](https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/foundational-ai-startup-funding-doubled-openai-anthropic-xai-q1-2026/)
**Source:** Crunchbase News · **Date:** 2026-03

Why this matters: Up to $50B from Amazon, $30B from NVIDIA, $30B from SoftBank — the round redefines what "AI infrastructure spend" looks like and ties OpenAI's future cap table to its three biggest compute counterparties. Strategic capital, not financial capital, is now the dominant force in frontier-lab financing.

## 2. [Anthropic in final stages of $50B round at $900B valuation](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/29/anthropic-weighs-raising-funds-at-900b-valuation-topping-openai.html)
**Source:** CNBC · **Date:** 2026-03 (talks active in March, reported in late April)

Why this matters: Anthropic's annualized revenue jumped from $9B at end-2025 to roughly $30B by March 2026, a 233% increase in one quarter driven primarily by Claude Code's enterprise traction. Valuation parity-plus with OpenAI is now plausible, not aspirational.

## 3. [NVIDIA projects $1 trillion in Blackwell + Vera Rubin orders through 2027](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/16/nvidia-gtc-2026-ceo-jensen-huang-keynote-blackwell-vera-rubin.html)
**Source:** CNBC · **Date:** 2026-03-16

Why this matters: Jensen Huang's GTC keynote put hard numbers on AI capex through 2027 — and committed NVIDIA's roadmap to that runway. The figure also gives investors and competitors (AMD, custom silicon) a target to plan against.

## 4. [Q1 2026 foundational-AI funding doubles all of 2025](https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/foundational-ai-startup-funding-doubled-openai-anthropic-xai-q1-2026/)
**Source:** Crunchbase News · **Date:** 2026-03

Why this matters: A single quarter exceeded the prior full year of foundational-AI venture funding, concentrated in OpenAI, Anthropic, and xAI. The capital is consolidating around incumbents — a meaningful signal for any "GPT-killer" startup pitch.

## 5. [Accenture + Databricks form joint business group for enterprise AI](https://newsroom.accenture.com/news/2026/accenture-and-databricks-accelerate-enterprise-adoption-of-ai-applications-and-agents-at-scale)
**Source:** Accenture Newsroom · **Date:** 2026-03-17

Why this matters: Big consulting + big data platform formalize what was already happening informally — a packaged path for Fortune 500s to ship agents in production on Databricks. The deal is a sign that AI services revenue (not API revenue) is where the next $100B will land.
