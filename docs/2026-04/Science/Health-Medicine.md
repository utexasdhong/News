# Health & Medicine — April 2026

## 1. [FDA approves Foundayo (orforglipron) — first oral GLP-1 for weight loss with no food restrictions](https://www.drugs.com/newdrugs.html)
**Source:** Drugs.com new approvals · **Date:** 2026-04-01

Why this matters: A daily oral GLP-1 that doesn't require fasting or water restrictions removes the biggest adherence barrier of the injectable class. Eli Lilly's orforglipron entering the market reshapes the GLP-1 competitive landscape against Novo Nordisk's Wegovy/Ozempic franchise — and dramatically expands the addressable patient population because of the pill convenience.

## 2. [FDA decision on Denali's tividenofusp alfa for Hunter syndrome](https://www.drugs.com/newdrugs.html)
**Source:** Drugs.com new approvals · **Date:** 2026-04-05

Why this matters: The first approved treatment for Hunter syndrome, a rare lysosomal storage disorder that causes progressive damage from infancy. Denali's brain-penetrant transport platform validates a broader class of CNS-targeted enzyme therapies — material for the entire rare-disease pipeline.

## 3. [FDA approves Idvynso (doravirine + islatravir) for HIV-1](https://www.drugs.com/newdrugs.html)
**Source:** Drugs.com new approvals · **Date:** 2026-04-21

Why this matters: A new fixed-dose oral combination for HIV-1 from Merck, designed for treatment-experienced adults. Adds a durable second-line option as HIV treatment continues its shift toward simpler regimens and toward long-acting formulations.

## 4. [Trump EO accelerates psychedelic-drug approvals for serious mental illness](https://www.morganlewis.com/pubs/2026/04/you-are-not-hallucinating-executive-order-aims-to-accelerate-approval-of-therapeutic-psychedelics)
**Source:** Morgan Lewis · **Date:** 2026-04-18

Why this matters: After FDA rejected MDMA-assisted therapy in 2024, the April 18 EO directs HHS/FDA to fast-track psilocybin, MDMA, and related compounds for PTSD, treatment-resistant depression, and serious mental illness. Material for psychedelic biotech (Compass Pathways, MindMed, Atai) and for VA mental-health treatment pathways.

## 5. [Fast-track designations cluster in oncology: ovarian, bladder, pancreatic combos](https://www.oncologynewscentral.com/drugs/info/oncology-drugs-fast-tracked-by-the-fda-in-april-2026)
**Source:** Oncology News Central · **Date:** 2026-04

Why this matters: FDA granted multiple fast-track designations in April, notably the lunresertib (PKMYT1 inhibitor) + zedoresertib (WEE1 inhibitor) combo for platinum-resistant ovarian cancer (April 20). Synthetic-lethality combos are finally producing real efficacy signals in the cancers that historically had the worst options.
