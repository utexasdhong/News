# Crypto & Fintech — April 2026

## 1. [Bitcoin posts best month since April 2025, climbing above $78,000](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/01/bitcoin-surged-in-april-but-weak-buyer-demand-makes-rally-vulnerable.html)
**Source:** CNBC · **Date:** 2026-05-01 (covering April)

Why this matters: BTC gained 12.7% in April, surpassing $78,000 mid-month — its best monthly performance in a year. But CNBC and on-chain analysts flagged that the rally was driven by leveraged derivatives activity while spot demand contracted, raising fragility concerns. The rally happened even with $80,000 acting as repeated resistance, with BTC retreating late month.

## 2. [Spot Bitcoin ETFs see ~$1.7B in net inflows — largest monthly total since October 2025](https://www.crowdfundinsider.com/2026/04/276289-bitcoin-and-crypto-markets-extend-recovery-in-april-2026-report-reveals/)

## 3. [SoFi becomes the first US chartered bank to offer direct digital-asset trading](https://www.crowdfundinsider.com/2026/04/276289-bitcoin-and-crypto-markets-extend-recovery-in-april-2026-report-reveals/)
**Source:** Crowdfund Insider · **Date:** 2026-04

Why this matters: A nationally chartered US bank offering direct crypto trading from deposit accounts is a regulatory milestone. The post-SAB 121 environment plus the new market-structure law have unlocked what Operation Choke Point 2.0 effectively prevented. Expect other charters to follow within the quarter.

## 4. [Tokenized US Treasuries hit record levels — BUIDL, USDY, OUSG, JTRSY all grow](https://www.crowdfundinsider.com/2026/04/276289-bitcoin-and-crypto-markets-extend-recovery-in-april-2026-report-reveals/)
**Source:** Crowdfund Insider · **Date:** 2026-04

Why this matters: BlackRock's BUIDL, Ondo's USDY/OUSG, and Janus Henderson's JTRSY each hit record AUM. Tokenized Treasuries are quietly becoming the most successful real-world-asset product in crypto — the on-chain yield substrate that stablecoin issuers and DAOs are using as collateral and reserve. The category has moved from "interesting experiment" to "scaled infrastructure."

## 5. [Square turns on Bitcoin payments for its entire US merchant base (ex-NY), free through 2026](https://www.crowdfundinsider.com/2026/04/276289-bitcoin-and-crypto-markets-extend-recovery-in-april-2026-report-reveals/)
**Source:** Crowdfund Insider · **Date:** 2026-04 (program launched 2026-03-30)

Why this matters: Block's Square enabled BTC acceptance by default — no opt-in for the merchant, zero fees through 2026 — across its entire US merchant base outside New York. The Lightning Network underpinning the rails is the actual test: this is the largest real-world deployment of consumer-facing BTC payments in the network's history.
