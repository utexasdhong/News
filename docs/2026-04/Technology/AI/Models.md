# Models — April 2026

## 1. [OpenAI ships GPT-5.5 ("Spud"), reclaiming the intelligence leaderboard](https://medium.com/@sanjeevpatel3007/april-2026-ai-models-every-major-release-reviewed-6ea03d7bc0b7)
**Source:** Medium (release roundup) · **Date:** 2026-04-23

Why this matters: GPT-5.5 is OpenAI's first fully retrained foundation model since the GPT-4.5 era — not an incremental patch. It jumped to #1 on Artificial Analysis's Intelligence Index with a score of 60 and posted 78.7% on OSWorld-Verified, the benchmark for autonomously navigating a real OS. The OS-level competence is the headline: agents that can actually operate computers are moving from demo to product.

## 2. [Google launches Gemini 3.1 Pro at one-third of GPT-5.4 Pro's cost](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/google-ai-updates-april-2026/)
**Source:** Google Blog · **Date:** 2026-04

Why this matters: Gemini 3.1 Pro leads 13 of 16 major benchmarks and ties GPT-5.4 Pro on the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index — at roughly a third of the API cost. The cost gap is now the real competitive story; capability parity at this price reshapes what every downstream app can afford to do per request.

## 3. [Anthropic confirms — and quietly withholds — Claude Mythos under "Project Glasswing"](https://renovateqr.com/blog/ai-models-april-2026)
**Source:** Renovate QR (release roundup) · **Date:** 2026-04-07

Why this matters: Anthropic announced on April 7 that Mythos — which posted 93.9% on SWE-bench Verified, 94.6% on GPQA Diamond, and reportedly surfaced thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities autonomously — will not be publicly released. Instead it's restricted to select partners under Project Glasswing for cybersecurity-risk reasons. First public instance of a frontier lab withholding a top model on safety grounds.

## 4. [DeepSeek unveils V4 Flash and V4 Pro flagships](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-24/deepseek-unveils-newest-flagship-a-year-after-ai-breakthrough)
**Source:** Bloomberg · **Date:** 2026-04-24

Why this matters: One year after DeepSeek R1 jolted Silicon Valley, the Hangzhou lab pushed V4 Flash and V4 Pro previews emphasizing coding and agentic reasoning. The release matters less for the benchmarks than for the cadence — Chinese labs are now shipping flagship-tier models on the same release rhythm as the US frontier, with continued open-weight availability.

## 5. [Anthropic releases Claude Opus 4.7; Meta drops Llama 4 Scout and Maverick](https://blog.mean.ceo/new-ai-model-releases-news-april-2026/)
**Source:** MEAN AI release tracker · **Date:** 2026-04

Why this matters: Opus 4.7 (April 16) continues Anthropic's roughly two-week production cadence focused on agentic coding reliability. Meta shipped Llama 4 Scout and Maverick on April 5, keeping the open-weights frontier credible. Taken together, April was the busiest release month of the year so far — every major lab pushed a flagship.
