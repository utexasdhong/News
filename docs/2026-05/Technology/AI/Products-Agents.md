# Products & Agents — May 2026

## 1. [Google introduces Gemini Spark — a 24/7 personal AI agent that runs even when devices are off](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/google-io-2026-all-our-announcements/)
**Source:** Google · **Date:** 2026-05-19

Why this matters: Spark is positioned as an always-on agent that navigates your digital life and takes action on your behalf, persisting in the background across phones and laptops. Beta access opens via Google AI Ultra. It's the boldest articulation yet of "ambient agent" as the next default product surface beyond chat.

## 2. [Microsoft Agent 365 hits general availability](https://futurumgroup.com/insights/microsoft-agent-365-turns-shadow-ai-into-a-governed-asset-class/)
**Source:** Futurum · **Date:** 2026-05-08

Why this matters: Agent 365 is Microsoft's unified control plane to observe, govern, and secure AI agents across the Microsoft and partner ecosystems. By turning "shadow AI" into a governed asset class, Microsoft is making the case that enterprise agent rollouts now need an identity-and-policy fabric — not just a model.

## 3. [Anthropic adds self-hosted sandboxes and MCP tunnels to Claude Managed Agents](https://aiagentstore.ai/ai-agent-news/this-week)
**Source:** Anthropic (via aiagentstore) · **Date:** 2026-05-19

Why this matters: Managed Agents got public-beta self-hosted sandboxes and a research-preview "MCP tunnels" feature letting agents reach internal MCP servers through an outbound-only encrypted gateway. This directly attacks the #1 enterprise objection to letting agents touch internal systems.

## 4. [ServiceNow and Accenture launch a Forward-Deployed Engineering program for agentic AI](https://newsroom.accenture.com/news/2026/servicenow-and-accenture-launch-forward-deployed-engineering-program-to-scale-agentic-ai-across-the-enterprise)
**Source:** Accenture · **Date:** 2026-05-06

Why this matters: ServiceNow's FDE team works inside customer environments with Accenture FDEs to take agentic workflows from pilot to production on the ServiceNow AI Platform. The OpenAI-style "forward-deployed engineer" model is now a mainstream enterprise-services template — a clear sign agents are leaving the demo phase.

## 5. [Kore.ai launches Agent Platform Artemis Edition for production multi-agent systems](https://www.helpnetsecurity.com/2026/05/22/kore-ai-unveils-ai-native-platform-for-enterprise-multiagent-systems/)
**Source:** Help Net Security · **Date:** 2026-05-22

Why this matters: Artemis lets enterprises deploy multi-agent systems "in days instead of months" with governance, observability, and operational controls enforced before any agent goes live. The competitive set for orchestration platforms is now Microsoft, ServiceNow, Salesforce, and a stack of focused vendors like Kore.ai.
