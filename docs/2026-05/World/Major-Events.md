# Major World Events — May 2026

## 1. [Strait of Hormuz blockade hardens — US Navy disables Iranian tankers](https://www.unitedagainstnucleariran.com/blog/iran-war-shipping-update-may-11-2026)
**Source:** UANI · **Date:** 2026-05-11

Why this matters: On May 8, US F/A-18s disabled the Iranian tankers M/T Sea Star III and M/T Sevda with precision munitions; on May 11 the Iraqi supertanker Agios Fanourios I reversed course rather than test the blockade line. Open transits through the strait have fallen to near zero since May 6, with 12 warships and 10,000+ personnel enforcing what CENTCOM calls "fully implemented." The world's most important oil chokepoint is effectively closed. ([Wikipedia background](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Strait_of_Hormuz_crisis))

## 2. [IEA chief: world facing "biggest energy crisis in history"](https://www.weforum.org/stories/2026/05/world-facing-biggest-ever-energy-crisis-and-more-top-energy-stories/)
**Source:** World Economic Forum · **Date:** 2026-05

Why this matters: The Hormuz blockade has produced fuel shortages in Europe and Asia, a US fuel deficit, and rerouted shipping around the Red Sea. Multiple energy ministers describe it as the largest energy-security threat since at least 1973 — bigger by some measures than the 2022 European gas crunch.

## 3. [Ukraine claws back 204 sq km from Russia over 30 days](https://www.russiamatters.org/news/russia-ukraine-war-report-card/russia-ukraine-war-report-card-may-20-2026)
**Source:** Russia Matters · **Date:** 2026-05-20

Why this matters: Russia registered net territorial losses for four straight weeks (April 21–May 19), reversing more than a year of slow Russian gains. Polls show 62% of Russians and 61% of Ukrainians now back negotiated settlements — but with diametrically opposed terms. The military reversal opens diplomatic space that wasn't there in 2025.

## 4. [Trump–Xi summit ends with public dispute over what was said](https://www.russiamatters.org/news/russia-ukraine-war-report-card/russia-ukraine-war-report-card-may-20-2026)
**Source:** Russia Matters · **Date:** 2026-05-15

Why this matters: The May 14–15 summit was the highest-level US–China meeting since the start of the Iran war. Trump publicly denied reports that Xi told him Putin would "regret" invading Ukraine. With Beijing positioned as the only credible neutral mediator, what's actually being said in private is consequential — and apparently leaking.

## 5. [Magnitude-6.9 earthquake strikes northern Chile near Calama](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_earthquakes_in_2026)
**Source:** Wikipedia / USGS · **Date:** 2026-05-25

Why this matters: The deep quake near the world's largest copper-producing region briefly disrupted mining and triggered landslides in the Antofagasta region. No deaths reported, but copper prices ticked up on supply concerns — a reminder of how concentrated critical-mineral supply has become.
