## 3. [Microsoft Copilot DLP bypass let AI read confidential emails since January](https://securityboulevard.com/2026/02/microsoft-patches-security-flaw-that-exposed-confidential-emails-to-ai/)
**Source:** Security Boulevard · **Date:** 2026-02

Why this matters: Copilot was summarizing emails labeled confidential by bypassing Microsoft 365's Data Loss Prevention controls, including drafts and sent folders. This is the canonical example of how AI assistants quietly expand the attack surface around enterprise data — and why DLP frameworks are being rewritten in 2026.

Why this matters: Six zero-days under active exploitation in a single month — across Windows SmartScreen, Office, IE legacy components, and Remote Desktop Services. Defenders had a hard month, and the persistent presence of IE-era code in 2026 patches is its own story about Windows legacy debt.

## 5. [BridgePay ransomware attack disrupts municipal payment systems](https://www.cm-alliance.com/cybersecurity-blog/february-2026-recent-cyber-attacks-data-breaches-ransomware-attacks)
