# AI Industry — February 2026

## 1. [Anthropic raises $30B Series G at $380B valuation](https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-raises-30-billion-series-g-funding-380-billion-post-money-valuation)
**Source:** Anthropic · **Date:** 2026-02-12

Why this matters: Anthropic more than doubled its valuation in months (from $183B to $380B post-money) on a $14B run-rate, with Claude Code alone at $2.5B. GIC and Coatue led; the round is the second-largest private financing in history. The signal: enterprise spend on AI has hit a level the venture market is now willing to capitalize against.

## 2. [February sets global VC record at $189B, driven by AI](https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/record-setting-global-funding-february-2026-openai-anthropic/)
**Source:** Crunchbase News · **Date:** 2026-02

Why this matters: Anthropic ($30B), OpenAI's pending mega-round, and Waymo's $16B alone totaled $156B — 83% of global VC for the month. While public software stocks reeled, private AI capital concentration reached historic levels. The bifurcation between public-market caution and private-market exuberance is now the defining capital-markets story.

## 3. [Nvidia reports record $68.1B Q4 revenue, up 73% YoY](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0001045810/000104581026000019/q4fy26pr.htm)
**Source:** Nvidia (SEC 8-K) · **Date:** 2026-02-25

Why this matters: Fiscal 2026 revenue hit $215.9B, up 65% year over year. Huang highlighted that Grace Blackwell already delivers an "order-of-magnitude lower cost per token" for inference. Token-economics — not training scale — is becoming the metric public markets price against.

## 4. [Snowflake unveils Cortex Code, a data-native AI coding agent](https://www.snowflake.com/en/news/press-releases/snowflake-unveils-cortex-code-an-ai-coding-agent-that-drastically-increases-productivity-by-understanding-your-enterprise-data-context/)
**Source:** Snowflake · **Date:** 2026-02-03

Why this matters: Cortex Code is a coding agent that operates inside Snowflake's data context rather than a generic IDE. The trend it represents — agents embedded in the data layer rather than the editor — is the enterprise-AI deployment pattern that will likely separate winners from losers.

## 5. [Tech selloff wipes ~$1T from software ETF as capex panic hits](https://mbrenndoerfer.com/writing/february-2026-selloff-anatomy-multi-trillion-dollar-wipeout)
**Source:** Brenndoerfer analysis · **Date:** 2026-02

Why this matters: Alphabet's $175–185B and Amazon's $200B 2026 capex disclosures sent stocks reeling. AMD's weak forecast triggered a 17% one-day drop. The narrative shift — from "AI will lift all boats" to "AI will destroy incumbent SaaS" — landed in markets in February, and it's still playing out.
