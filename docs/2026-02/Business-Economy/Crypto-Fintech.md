# Crypto & Fintech — February 2026

## 1. [Bitcoin crashes to ~$60K, down 50% from October high](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/bitcoin-price-today-70000-in-focus.html)
**Source:** CNBC · **Date:** 2026-02-05

Why this matters: A Feb 5 cascade of liquidations briefly took BTC below $61,000 — down 15% on the day and roughly 50% off the October $125K peak. The combination of tariff-driven risk-off, levered position unwinds, and broader crypto-doubt headlines marked the deepest correction of this cycle so far.

## 2. [Coindesk: Bitcoin's $60K February selloff likely marked the cycle bottom](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/05/20/these-bitcoin-metrics-suggest-february-s-usd60-000-selloff-may-have-marked-the-bottom)
**Source:** CoinDesk · **Date:** 2026-02

Why this matters: Persistent negative perpetual funding rates and onchain capitulation signals during February now look (in hindsight) like a textbook bottom. ETF inflows resumed at multi-month highs into Q2, reinforcing the read that institutional positioning, not retail flow, is the marginal price-setter for BTC in 2026.

## 3. [BridgePay ransomware attack disrupts city government payment systems](https://www.cm-alliance.com/cybersecurity-blog/february-2026-recent-cyber-attacks-data-breaches-ransomware-attacks)
**Source:** Cyber Management Alliance · **Date:** 2026-02

Why this matters: A mid-tier payments processor with heavy municipal exposure was knocked offline, cascading outages into dozens of cities. The episode is a case study in fintech vendor concentration risk — and the soft underbelly of public-sector digital payments infrastructure.

## 4. [Bitcoin bounces to $67K mid-month before resuming decline](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/bitcoin-price-today-crypto-volatility.html)
**Source:** CNBC · **Date:** 2026-02-11

Why this matters: A brief two-week rebound to ~$67K failed to hold above $64K by Feb 24 as tariff fears intensified again. The intra-month volatility profile (~10–15% intraday ranges) makes February one of the highest realized-volatility months for BTC in over a year.
