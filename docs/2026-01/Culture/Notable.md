## 3. [Notable deaths: Gladys West, GPS pioneer](https://www.britannica.com/topic/Deaths-in-2026)
**Source:** Britannica (Deaths in 2026) · **Date:** 2026-01-19

Why this matters: West's mathematical work on Earth modeling was foundational to the Global Positioning System. Her career — as a Black woman mathematician at the US Naval Surface Warfare Center starting in the 1950s — is one of the underrecognized stories in the history of how the modern world actually got built.

## 4. [Notable deaths: Roger Allers, "The Lion King" co-director](https://www.britannica.com/topic/Deaths-in-2026)
**Source:** Britannica (Deaths in 2026) · **Date:** 2026-01-17

Why this matters: Allers co-directed the 1994 film that defined Disney's 90s renaissance and reshaped what mainstream animation could be. His passing closes a chapter on a generation of directors who built the modern animated blockbuster.

## 5. [Notable deaths: Claudette Colvin, civil rights pioneer](https://www.britannica.com/topic/Deaths-in-2026)
**Source:** Britannica (Deaths in 2026) · **Date:** 2026-01-17

Why this matters: Colvin was arrested at 15 in 1955 for refusing to give up her bus seat to a white woman — nine months before Rosa Parks. Her story is the canonical example of how civil rights history was simplified for a mass audience, and her death is an opportunity to revisit who really moved the moment.
