# Major World Events — January 2026

## 1. [US military captures Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_United_States_intervention_in_Venezuela)
**Source:** Wikipedia (2026 US intervention in Venezuela) · **Date:** 2026-01-03

Why this matters: Operation Absolute Resolve — over 150 aircraft involved, US Armed Forces bombed northern Venezuela air defenses, apprehension force took Maduro from his Caracas compound at 2 a.m. He was on a US carrier en route to New York by 4:29 a.m. The single most consequential geopolitical event of January. ([CBS coverage](https://www.cbsnews.com/live-updates/venezuela-us-military-strikes-maduro-trump/))

## 2. [Iran's deadliest crackdown since the 1979 revolution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal:Current_events/January_2026)
**Source:** Wikipedia Current Events · **Date:** 2026-01

Why this matters: Nationwide protests over economic conditions met with state violence on a scale not seen since the Islamic Republic was founded. Reported death tolls ranged from 12,000 to 30,000. A regime legitimacy crisis at this scale reshapes oil markets, regional alliances, and migration patterns.

## 4. [Bulgaria adopts the euro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal:Current_events/January_2026)
**Source:** Wikipedia Current Events · **Date:** 2026-01-01

Why this matters: The 21st eurozone member, and the first new entrant in years. During a period of EU friction with both the US and its own industry over CBAM, a member-state joining the common currency is a structural cohesion data point in the other direction.

## 5. [Uganda's general election: Museveni wins amid fraud allegations](https://www.controlrisks.com/our-thinking/geopolitical-calendar)
**Source:** Control Risks · **Date:** 2026-01-15

Why this matters: One of Africa's longest-running personalist regimes extends its grip with credible reports of irregularities. The election is a marker of broader democratic backsliding across the region — and a preview of how contested transitions there may eventually unfold.
