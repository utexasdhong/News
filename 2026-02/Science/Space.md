# Space — February 2026

## 1. [NASA restructures Artemis: III becomes LEO demo, IV becomes first crewed Moon landing](https://www.nasa.gov/news-release/nasa-adds-mission-to-artemis-lunar-program-updates-architecture/)
**Source:** NASA · **Date:** 2026-02-27

Why this matters: Administrator Jared Isaacman confirmed the biggest Artemis architecture change since the program began. Artemis III in 2027 becomes a low-Earth-orbit test of one or both commercial landers (SpaceX, Blue Origin); the first crewed lunar landing slips to Artemis IV in 2028. SLS Block 1B and Block 2 cancelled; Gateway paused. A wholesale return to "test in LEO first" philosophy.

## 2. [Ariane 64 makes successful maiden flight with 32 Amazon Leo satellites](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_in_spaceflight)
**Source:** Wikipedia · **Date:** 2026-02-12

Why this matters: First flight of Ariane 6's four-booster variant, and the first successful orbital maiden flight of 2026. Importantly, it puts Europe's heavy-lift launcher into operational service for Amazon's Project Kuiper constellation — a counterweight to SpaceX Falcon 9 dominance in the LEO mega-constellation business.

## 3. [SpaceX postpones Mars ambitions "5–7 years" to prioritize lunar missions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_in_spaceflight)
**Source:** Wikipedia · **Date:** 2026-02-09

Why this matters: Musk's public reset of Starship's timeline. Mars-by-2030 has been the implicit roadmap since 2016; pushing it out to ~2031–2033 to focus on Artemis support is a major strategic concession to NASA's customer relationship and a tacit acknowledgment of Starship development pace.

## 4. [NASA cancels SLS Block 1B/Block 2, pauses Lunar Gateway](https://www.nasa.gov/directorates/esdmd/nasa-strengthens-artemis-adds-mission-refines-overall-architecture/)
**Source:** NASA · **Date:** 2026-02-27

Why this matters: Two of the costliest planned program upgrades — Exploration Upper Stage and the Gateway lunar station — were shelved in a single announcement. Standardizing on SLS Block 1 with a Centaur V upper stage is the largest budget reallocation in NASA's exploration program in over a decade.
