# US Politics — February 2026

## 1. [Supreme Court strikes down Trump's IEEPA tariffs 6-3](https://www.scotusblog.com/2026/02/supreme-court-strikes-down-tariffs/)
**Source:** SCOTUSblog · **Date:** 2026-02-20

Why this matters: Chief Justice Roberts, writing for a 6-3 majority (Sotomayor, Kagan, Gorsuch, Barrett, and Jackson joining), held IEEPA does not authorize presidential tariffs. With more than $200B in tariffs collected since 2025, the refund question is now the major open issue. The single most consequential domestic political-economy ruling of early 2026.

## 2. [Trump invokes Section 122 to impose 15% global tariff post-ruling](https://www.cnn.com/politics/live-news/trump-administration-news-02-23-26)
**Source:** CNN Politics · **Date:** 2026-02-22

Why this matters: Hours after the IEEPA ruling, Trump announced a 10% global tariff under Section 122 of the 1974 Trade Act, then raised it to 15% — Section 122's maximum. The executive's trade-power workaround within a week of losing at the Court shows the legal/political contest over tariff authority is now structural rather than episodic.
