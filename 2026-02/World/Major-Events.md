# Major World Events — February 2026

## 1. [BNP wins Bangladesh general election in landslide, first post-Hasina vote](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/2/13/bangladesh-election-results-2026-who-won-who-lost-whats-next)
**Source:** Al Jazeera · **Date:** 2026-02-12

Why this matters: First election since Sheikh Hasina was ousted by student-led protests in August 2024. BNP under Tarique Rahman took 208 of 300 seats (two-thirds supermajority). Jamaat-e-Islami emerged as main opposition with 69 — its best showing ever. Turnout 59.88%. The Awami League was barred from competing. The political realignment of South Asia's third-largest democracy is now formalized.

## 2. [New START Treaty between US and Russia expires](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal:Current_events/February_2026)
**Source:** Wikipedia Current Events · **Date:** 2026-02-05

Why this matters: For the first time since 1972, no bilateral US-Russia treaty limits strategic nuclear arsenals. With INF and Open Skies already gone, the strategic arms-control architecture that defined Cold War end-game stability has effectively collapsed in a single decade.

## 3. [Supreme Court strikes down Trump's IEEPA tariffs 6-3](https://www.scotusblog.com/2026/02/supreme-court-strikes-down-tariffs/)
**Source:** SCOTUSblog · **Date:** 2026-02-20

Why this matters: The most consequential US ruling of early 2026 — voiding the legal basis for over $200B in tariffs. Global trade partners awaited refund/reciprocity decisions. Trump's Section 122 workaround at 15% kept the trade-war posture intact, but the precedent against expansive executive economic emergency authority is durable.

## 4. [Trump pushes one-month deadline on Ukraine peace deal, presses Donbas concessions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_United_States%E2%80%93Ukraine%E2%80%93Russia_meetings_in_Geneva)
**Source:** Wikipedia · **Date:** 2026-02-25

Why this matters: Trump told Zelenskyy by phone he wanted the war ended within a month. Zelenskyy publicly disclosed both Washington and Moscow were pressuring Ukraine to cede the remainder of Donbas. Multi-round talks in the UAE and Switzerland produced no territorial breakthrough. The shape of any future settlement is now openly on Russia's terms.

## 5. [Rob Jetten sworn in as youngest, first openly gay Dutch PM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal:Current_events/February_2026)
**Source:** Wikipedia Current Events · **Date:** 2026-02-23

Why this matters: At 38, Jetten took office after extended coalition talks following the November 2025 election. The Netherlands installing a young, centrist, openly gay PM as much of Europe trends right is a notable counter-data point — and reshapes the EU's internal coalition math on migration and climate.
